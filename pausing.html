How I Realized Pausing Is Necessary

Posted on February 18, 2017

Whenever I'm playing guitar and trying to come up with a new sound or trying to hit a deadline for my editor, I'm hit with the intense feeling of pressure. 'Finish, finish, finish!', my brain tells me. And my brain's not wrong: I need to hurry up and finish so I can get my life moving. But recently I've come to the realization that pausing is necessary. Yes, that's right, pausing.
I've been reading articles on meditation recently, and clearing my mind before doing any heavy lifting. They talk about how the brain works out just like any other muscle and sometimes it needs a rest or a pause. If you take a moment to breathe and meditate, you can get rid of the noise. That toxic back talk your brain gives you. Sometimes it's loud and overpowering. Not necessarily 'finish, finish, finish' but just general noise. Taking a moment to pause and let that noise run its course has helped me to be overcome some anxieties I've had about not only deadlines, but life in general.
