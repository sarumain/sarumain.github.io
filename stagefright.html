Challenging Stage Fright

Posted on February 2, 2017

Stage fright sucks.
There's no doubt about it but you gotta get over it.
When I first started playing music in front of a crowd, it was just my family. There wasn't anything to be afraid. But then I started doing it in high school and I was nervous. I got through it, but I was nervous.
How is it possible I can love playing music but not in front of people. Here's my secret to getting over it....just do it. I know, cliche advice. But just walking up those stairs onto the platform and it does the trick. I don't think about stage fright when I'm on the stage. The most it comes from is when I'm off. It should be called off stage fright, really.
