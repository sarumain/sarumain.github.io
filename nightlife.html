Why Nightlife Can Be Good & Bad

Posted on January 13, 2017

This is something I've been thinking about lately but not really in any sphere of importance.
A few of my friends like to go out, which isn't anything particularily out of the norm. But there's a lot of times, maybe even most of the time, where I don't want to go out. I just want to stay in, watch TV, maybe read a book. Don't get me wrong, I'm not that much of an introvert. But I don't like nightlife. Give me a brunch date or an afternoon hangout. Nights feel like they belong in a specific place where staying in seems better.
