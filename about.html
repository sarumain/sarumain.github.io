About Me

I'm a musician in Austin, Texas. 26-years-old.
That about sums it up but I also graduated in Communications from University of Texas in 2011. I learned shortly afterwards that music was my only purpose in life so I pursued it and the music scene in Austin is vibrant enough that here I am five years later playing music. I'm great with a guitar and a set of drums, having taken lessons since I was 15. My favorite band of all time is Rush, but I've got a soft spot for Radiohead.
